Welcome to our Monthly Jam!

This is the monthly game jam. If you already know what a Game Jam is and the rules, then you can jump here.

How do I rate the games

I rate the games by three qualities:

  • Quality
  • Creativity
  • If it follows the rules

I give each of the qulities a scale from 0 to 10. And the add those scales to get a score. The tiers:

  • A score from 27 to 30 mans your game is featured. When you get this, you will be reconized in the "Games" section of this website.
  • A score from 24 to 26 means your game is good, but not the best I played.
  • A score from 21 to 25 means your game is average.
  • A score from 20 to 18 means you have a lot to improve in your game
  • A score 17 or below means that your game fails

Rules

  • Games can't include any inappropiate stuff, such as gore, threats, or personal information.
  • Games can't be larger than 20KB
  • Games can't be generated by AI (Artificial Intelligence)
  • Games should stick to the corrisponding theme. (If that rule didn't exist, then the monthly game jams would be no fun.)
  • Your game has to be original.
  • If I have to download your game, your game should be found in an .sb3 or a .html extension.
